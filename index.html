FAQ

What is ReflectQ?

ReflectQ is an AI-powered self-reflection and daily journaling application designed to help users write personal reflections, understand their emotions, and receive personalized reflection content and Quranic verse recommendations to support personal growth and spiritual reflection. ReflectQ serves as a reflection companion and is not a substitute for professional, medical, or religious advice.

How does the AI feature work in ReflectQ?

ReflectQ’s AI analyzes the reflection input provided by users to generate Quranic verse recommendations, follow-up reflection prompts, and personalized reflective insights. The AI provides guidance and suggestions only and does not make absolute judgments or final decisions. Users remain fully in control of how the recommendations are used.

Does ReflectQ provide religious or psychological advice?

All reflection content and recommendations in ReflectQ are intended to support personal reflection and self-awareness based on the Quran. Quranic verses are referenced from official sources such as eQuran and publications by the Indonesian Ministry of Religious Affairs. These recommendations are provided for reflection purposes only and do not constitute authoritative religious rulings, interpretations, or professional psychological advice.

What data does ReflectQ collect?

ReflectQ collects data necessary to provide its services, including account information (such as email, name, and phone number), reflection entries submitted by users, application usage data, and limited device information. Payment data is not stored by ReflectQ and is fully processed by Apple.

Is my reflection data private and secure?

Yes. User reflection data is treated as private and protected using industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit and restricted internal access. ReflectQ does not share user reflection data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Does ReflectQ share my data with third parties?

ReflectQ uses third-party services such as Supabase, Firebase, OpenAI, Google Gemini, and eQuran solely to support application functionality. Data sharing is limited to technical necessities and is subject to each provider’s respective privacy policies.

How can I change the app theme?

You can change the app theme by tapping the color palette icon on the Home screen and selecting either Light Mode or Dark Mode.

How can I change the app language?

You can change the app language by tapping the language icon on the Home screen and selecting your preferred language. ReflectQ supports Indonesian, English, Spanish, French, and Arabic.

What is the Premium feature and how do I subscribe?

The Premium feature provides exclusive content designed to help users gain deeper insights into their spiritual journey. To subscribe, go to Profile > Premium and select your preferred plan. Payments are processed via Apple In-App Purchase. ReflectQ does not store payment information, and all transactions are handled securely by Apple.

How can I contact support?

You can contact our support team via email or WhatsApp through the Profile > Contact Us menu. Our team will be happy to assist you.

Can ReflectQ be used offline?

Currently, ReflectQ requires an active internet connection. Internet access is necessary for data processing, account synchronization, AI-powered features, and third-party services.

How can I change my account password?

To change your password, go to Profile > Profile Info, enter your current password, new password, and password confirmation, then tap Change Password to save your changes.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

If you forget your password, tap the Forgot Password button on the login screen and enter your registered email address. ReflectQ will send you a password reset link via email. After setting a new password, you can log in again.

Can I cancel my subscription? Is there a refund?

You can cancel your Premium subscription via Profile > Premium or through your Apple ID account settings at any time. Cancellation will stop automatic renewal for the next billing period. Payments already made are non-refundable in accordance with Apple’s policies. Premium access remains active until the end of the subscription period.

Can I delete my account and personal data?

Yes. You can request account deletion through the Delete Account feature in the app or by contacting ReflectQ support via email or WhatsApp. To delete your account through the app, go to Profile > Profile Info and select Delete Account. After confirmation, your account will be inaccessible and your data will be retained for up to 30 days before permanent deletion. During this period, you may contact support to restore your account if needed.
